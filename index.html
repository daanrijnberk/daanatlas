About us
We do the planning for you!

We are here to help you with your vacation to colombia by providing you with
all te tips and information required. Hotels, locations, trips and rental.
We provide it all.

We are a start-up, started in 2010. We are a extremely motivated group of people,
all with a passion for Colombia and its beautifull culture, people and nature.
Colombia offers a huge variety in all things. You can go skiing of get lost in
a rainforrest. There is plenty of hidden gems and romantic places all about.
Yet Colombia is not the most relaxed country in the world. There are some things
to think about and to be aware of. Some places are to be avoided, because they are
simply not safe. But thats why we are here. We will make sure you plan the right things
and help you avoid the wrong places. We will do this with tips and advice, all to be found on our website.

We can also help you organise your whole vacation. We have partnerships with the biggest airlines in the world to guarantee you the best prices and the best seats. We also partner up with hotels and rental in Colombia for the best quality for the best price. This all can be arranged through us. This way we can offer you a worry free vacation where everything is planned for you. In this case we will also be your contact when something goes wrong. We will find the best solution for you within ours.
