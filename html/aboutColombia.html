The map on the left shows the most exclusive, hidden and special locations.
These locations have been carefully selected out of all the great places
Colombia has to offer. All of these locations are tourist friendly and offer
no worries at all. In case of conflict or other bad situations locations will
be removed from our list. This way we keep our selected locations the best no
matter the circumstances.

All locations differ in type. You can click the locations on the map to receive
more information about the spot. In general we filtered them into three different
groups: Nature, City, other. The location indicators can give you more information
like name and description.
